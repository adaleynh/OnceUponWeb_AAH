The moment I knew she was the one.

💕 Meeting Latte

The shelter was quiet, filled with tiny meows. We were sure we’d get a tortie, but then we saw them—two tiny ginger American Shorthairs curled up together, barely awake.

When I reached for one, she wiggled away, leaving her sleepy sister behind. The staff handed her to me, and the moment I held her, I knew... she was the one.
